Why Us

We're not lawyers.
We're navigators.

Relocating to a new country is one of life's biggest transitions. We created Canadian Immigration Concierge to make that transition simpler, more supported, and more human — especially for the growing number of Americans ready to start a new chapter in Canada.

While immigration lawyers handle the legal side, we handle everything else — the practical, the personal, and the day-to-day. Our multilingual team of dedicated advisors is available remotely, wherever you are and wherever in Canada you're headed.

Where legal representation is needed, we refer you to licensed professionals. Referrals are always free. We work in formal partnership with a network of licensed Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultants (RCICs) and immigration lawyers.
