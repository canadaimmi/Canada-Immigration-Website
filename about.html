Our Story

More than logistics —
it's life.

Relocating to a new country is one of life's biggest transitions. We created Canadian Immigration Concierge to make that transition simpler, more supported, and more human.

We've seen firsthand how stressful it can be to navigate a new country — the housing market, the healthcare system, the schools, the paperwork — without someone in your corner who knows the ropes. That's exactly what we provide.

Our team of multilingual advisors brings together expertise across housing, education, healthcare, community integration, and more. We operate entirely remotely, which means we can serve clients anywhere in the world, moving to anywhere in Canada.
